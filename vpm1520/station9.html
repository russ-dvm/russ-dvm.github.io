Question 1: Describe the lesions.

Brain: the cerebellum is diffusely decreased in size/volume/mass. Kidney: The kidney is markedly small, but appears to have normal architecture.

Question 2: What disturbance of growth is illustrated here?

Hypoplasia.

Question 3: What are your morphological diagnoses?

Brain: cerebellar hypoplasia; Kidney: renal hypoplasia.
